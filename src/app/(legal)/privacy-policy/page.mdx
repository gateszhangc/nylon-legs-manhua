# Privacy Policy for Nylon Legs

## Overview
This Privacy Policy explains what information **Nylon Legs** collects, why it is collected, and how it is used.

## Information We Collect
We collect only the information needed to operate the Service:

1. **Access and session data**
   - locale preference, age-gate confirmation state, browser session identifiers, and similar technical preferences.

2. **Technical and diagnostic data**
   - request logs, browser type, device type, coarse IP-derived region, error events, and performance signals used for reliability and abuse prevention.

3. **Support and rights review communications**
   - information you send when contacting us for support, correction, or rights review.

4. **Optional account or payment data**
   - if paid access or account features are enabled later, we may collect account and billing details required to operate those features. Payment processing is handled by third-party providers.

## How We Use Information
- deliver localized pages and age-gated reading flows;
- maintain site reliability and security;
- respond to support, correction, and rights-related requests;
- enforce our Terms of Service and content restrictions;
- comply with legal obligations.

## Cookies
The site may use cookies or equivalent browser storage to remember:
- age-gate confirmation;
- language preference;
- session state required for site functionality.

## Sharing
We do not sell personal data. We share information only with service providers needed to host or secure the site, or when disclosure is required by law or necessary to protect rights and platform integrity.

## Retention
We keep technical logs and communications only as long as reasonably needed for operations, security, legal compliance, and rights handling.

## Your Choices
- You can clear browser cookies to remove stored age-gate and language preferences.
- You can contact us to request review or deletion of support communications, subject to legal retention requirements.
